Welcome to Swami Vivekanand Vidhya Peeth, Alirajpur

At Swami Vivekanand Vidhya Peeth, Alirajpur, we believe that education is the foundation for a better future. Inspired by the timeless values and visionary ideals of Swami Vivekananda, our institution is dedicated to nurturing young minds with knowledge, character, and purpose.

Established with a mission to provide quality education rooted in Indian culture and global excellence, our school fosters a stimulating learning environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and confidence. We emphasize not just academic achievement, but also the holistic development of every child— intellectually, morally, socially, and physically.

Our dedicated team of educators, modern infrastructure, and a student-centric approach ensure that every learner receives personalized guidance and the tools they need to succeed in a dynamic world.

"Where values meet vision, and learning shapes leaders."

Welcome to Swami Vivekanand Vidhya Peeth — empowering minds, enriching lives.

About Our School

Swami Vivekanand Vidhya Peeth, Alirajpur is a center of academic excellence and character development, committed to shaping the leaders of tomorrow. Founded with the vision of creating a vibrant learning environment, our school is grounded in the inspirational philosophy of Swami Vivekananda — fostering education that builds strength, wisdom, and integrity.

Located in the heart of Alirajpur, our school offers a blend of traditional values and modern education. We strive to provide a well-rounded education that goes beyond textbooks, nurturing each child’s physical, emotional, social, and intellectual potential.

Our curriculum is designed to meet national standards while incorporating innovative teaching methods and real-world learning. Equipped with modern classrooms, laboratories, libraries, and sports facilities, the school provides an ideal setting for holistic development.

At Swami Vivekanand Vidhya Peeth, education is not just about academic success, but about building responsible citizens, lifelong learners, and compassionate human beings. We take pride in our dedicated faculty, supportive community, and the bright, curious minds we guide every day.

Join us in our journey to ignite minds, inspire hearts, and impact the future.
